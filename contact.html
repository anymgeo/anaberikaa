Ana Berikashvili

Ana Berikashvili is a successful entrepreneur and the owner of Anaberika Winery. She is also a graduate of GIPA (Georgian Institute of Public Affairs) with a degree in Public Administration. Ana's journey in the wine industry began with a passion for viticulture and a desire to create exceptional wines.

After completing her studies at GIPA, Ana ventured into the world of winemaking. She founded Anaberika Winery, which has since gained recognition for producing high-quality wines. The winery is known for its dedication to traditional winemaking methods while also embracing innovation and modern techniques.

Throughout her career, Ana has demonstrated a deep understanding of the intricacies of winemaking, from grape cultivation to fermentation and aging processes. She has honed her expertise in crafting unique and expressive wines that showcase the richness and diversity of Georgian terroir.

Ana Berikashvili's commitment to excellence has earned her a reputation as a respected figure in the wine industry. Her wines have garnered accolades both domestically and internationally, contributing to the overall growth and recognition of Georgian wines on the global stage.

In addition to her entrepreneurial pursuits, Ana actively participates in industry events, conferences, and wine exhibitions. She engages with fellow winemakers, industry professionals, and wine enthusiasts to promote Georgian wines and share her knowledge and experiences. As a prominent figure in the wine industry, Ana Berikashvili continues to contribute to the development and promotion of Georgian winemaking, combining her passion for wine with her expertise in public administration.
