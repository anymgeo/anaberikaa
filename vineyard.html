Trip to Anaberika's vineyard

Welcome to our picturesque vineyard, where nature's beauty intertwines with the art of winemaking. Step into a world of sensory delights as you immerse yourself in our vineyard's enchanting ambiance.

Indulge in the symphony of flavors that await you, as our passionate winemaker crafts the finest wines. Our vineyard boasts an impressive selection of varietals, including Kisi, Rkatsiteli, Mtsvane, Rkatsiteli Mtsvane, Saperavi, and Luna. Each wine is meticulously crafted, showcasing the unique characteristics of the local terroir and the expertise of our winemaking team. From the rich reds that dance on your palate to the crisp whites that refresh your senses, each sip tells a story of dedication and expertise.

Unwind and relax in our inviting outdoor terrace, where you can bask in the gentle breeze while relishing a carefully curated lunch menu. Our talented chefs are passionate about showcasing the flavors of the region. They skillfully craft delectable dishes using locally sourced ingredients, ensuring a farm-to-table experience that celebrates the bounty of the land. For those seeking traditional Kakhetian dishes, we offer a range of options such as Badrijani (eggplant rolls with walnut paste), Mtsvadi (grilled meat skewers), and Khinkali (dumplings filled with meat or vegetarian options). We also cater to vegan and gluten-free dietary preferences, offering a variety of delicious plant-based dishes that are sure to delight.

As you dine, each dish is thoughtfully paired with our wines, creating a harmonious fusion of flavors. Whether you're enjoying a glass of our velvety Saperavi with a perfectly seared steak or savoring the delicate notes of our Mtsvane alongside a refreshing salad, the combination of food and wine elevates your senses to new heights.

Immerse yourself in the beauty of our vineyard, where every aspect is designed to create a memorable experience. From the moment you arrive, you'll be greeted by stunning vistas, lush vineyards, and a sense of tranquility that envelops the air. Whether you're a wine enthusiast or simply seeking a moment of respite, our vineyard offers a sanctuary where you can connect with nature, indulge in exceptional wines, and savor the flavors of Kakhetian cuisine. Come join us in this sensory journey and discover the magic that awaits you at our vineyard.
